Hello!

About Me:
I’m a Higher Secondary student in Bangladesh with a passion for Computer Science. I noticed that finding high-quality educational videos on YouTube often required guessing the right keywords, and even then, results were inconsistent. That inspired me to build an app that ranks videos using a custom scoring algorithm.

About This App:
This app tries to filter through the millions of videos available on youtube using youtube api in order to get the best recommendation following a scoring algorithm I developed. It takes the following things into consideration:

  1. Relevancy
  2. Duration
  3. Upload Date
  4. Engagement
  5. Channel Quality
Based on the above metrics, it gives a score on each video on a weighted basis, and then it orders them to find the best from all. However, like all algorithm, it has its flaws. It might not always generate the best video. It might also show different video of a different Class as the top video. However, we tried our best to find the top videos and really hope that the best video is included in the full list beyond the top reccomendation.

How is this different from searching in youtube?:
Glad you asked. While this searching might feel like a youtube search with buttons instead of typing, but the filtering of videos is done on a different process than the youtube search. Because, a youtube search is often biased on promotion, engagement, and hype instead of quality, it often isn't suitable for an "academic" search. Since youtube search algorithm is much more broad, it will often not be focused on academics. And we all know about the torments of ads we need to tolerate when searching for a simple video, this app removes those ads too!
This app primarily aims to reduce the thinking friction and distraction among the students to find the class for their specific videos!
